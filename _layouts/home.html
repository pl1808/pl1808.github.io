Welcome to The Albatross Club

We provide members with all the fun and enjoyment of racehorse ownership coupled with a series of exclusive annual events.

Success

An invitation only racing club with a track record of success.

Syndicates

Combined flat and national hunt syndicates.

Events

Annual luxury events schedule with great networking opportunities.

Trainers + Jockeys

Working with the world premier trainers and retained jockeys.

Celebrity

Winning

A focus on winning and all round fun with potential high returns.

Membership

Another level

The Albatross Club is unique membership, which brings the enjoyment and passion of syndicated racehorse ownership experience to another level.

Racehorse ownership

As a member you will become part of a syndicate that owns two horses, both flat and national hunt stabled with two of the country's leading trainers.

Stable tours

You will be invited to exclusive stable tours, to take part in golf days staged on some of the country's best golf courses, and enjoy fine dining and social experiences.

Celebrity

You will have access to a host of sports stars both past and present at our events and be able to read and watch exclusive interviews from our ambassadors, who include classic winning jockey Frankie Dettori, Grade 1 winning Jockey Jim Crowley, Channel 4's Grand Nation Wining Jockey Jim Crowley and, former Open Champion Darren Clarke and Ryder Cup golfer Lee Westwood.
